ABOUT US


Our team is full of creators and collectors alike, so we’ve built a platform where the community can come together and thrive
UCOLLEX is an innovative platform that sells unique and limited edition NFTs for toy and pop culture collectibles. Founded by artists who are also collectors themselves, we want to offer NFTs in an accessible way to everyone. A place where collectors can feel at home and live their passion for collecting through exclusive content, interviews with artists, the best 3D art, and innovative VR experiences.
An NFT is a digital asset that represents real-world objects like art, music, in-game items, videos and are becoming an increasingly popular way to buy and sell digital artwork. They are bought and sold online, frequently with cryptocurrency, and are generally encoded with the same underlying software as many cryptocurrencies.
We want as many individuals to enjoy the wonderful of digital art as possible so UCOLLEX lets you purchase digital art using various Fiat Payment Methods and allows users to choose the corresponding ones based on their currencies or region. You can buy your first NFT with your credit card.
All you need is a mobile phone number or email to create your account in 3 steps. We've made it super easy to get started.
